My Blog

Apr 15 2020

Today I will be talking about Valorant. Valorant is a good game. I like it. My favorite character to play at the moment is Omen. His ult cannot be used with abandon, but it can be used effectively to reinforce teammates or to make sneaky plays. And of course, his smokes are instrumental in the success of the team.

Playing with a shitty team kills my soul. When I play on an unranked ladder, I hope that the enemy team has as many shitty team members as my team has. If this is the case, then I can make plays. Top-fragging still a given with ya boy.

Do I see the game as e-sports worthy? Who knows. Before anybody can answer that question, ranked matchmaking needs to be introduced and a meta needs to be crafted. Whether or not a game is e-sports worthy depends on two factors: balance and watchability. While I have no doubt that the game will be watchable on a professional level, the question of balance comes to mind. However, I am leaning towards believing that a balance is reachable.
